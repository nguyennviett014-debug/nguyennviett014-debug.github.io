Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

VidFlow ("Service", "we", "us") is a video management and cross-platform publishing automation tool. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you use VidFlow to manage and publish video content to social media platforms.

1. Information We Collect

Authentication Tokens
We store OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens for the following platforms that you choose to connect:

  • TikTok — for video upload via the TikTok Content Posting API
  • YouTube — for video upload and playlist management
  • Facebook — for video upload to authorized Pages
  • Instagram — for video content publishing

These tokens are stored locally in configuration files on your own server. They are never transmitted to VidFlow's developers or any third-party server operated by us.

Video Content and Metadata
VidFlow processes video files that you download from permitted sources and re-publish to your authorized platform accounts. Video files are temporarily stored on your server during processing. We also store video metadata (titles, descriptions, upload history, view counts) in a local SQLite database on your server.

Usage Logs
VidFlow writes operational logs to your local server for debugging and monitoring purposes. These logs may include video IDs, upload status, and API response codes, but do not include personal user data beyond what you explicitly configure.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use authentication tokens solely to:

  • Upload video content to platform accounts you have authorized
  • Manage playlists and video metadata on your behalf
  • Retrieve basic analytics (view counts, watch time) for performance tracking
  • Refresh access tokens when they expire, using stored refresh tokens

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, sale to third parties, or any purpose beyond the direct operation of VidFlow on your behalf.

3. TikTok API Data

VidFlow uses the TikTok API (Content Posting API) to upload videos to TikTok accounts. When you connect a TikTok account, VidFlow:

  • Requests only the permissions necessary for video upload (video.upload)
  • Stores your TikTok access token locally on your server only
  • Does not access, collect, or store your TikTok followers, direct messages, comments, or personal profile data beyond what is required for upload confirmation
  • Does not use TikTok user data for any purpose other than publishing video content on your explicit behalf

TikTok user data obtained through the TikTok API will not be shared with any third party, sold, rented, or used for advertising purposes. Such data is used solely to enable video publishing functionality as directed by the authorized account holder.

4. Data Storage and Security

All data processed by VidFlow is stored locally on your own server infrastructure. VidFlow does not operate centralized servers that receive or store your authentication tokens, video files, or personal data.

  • OAuth tokens are stored in local configuration files on your server
  • Video processing occurs entirely on your server
  • Database records remain on your server's local filesystem
  • No user data is transmitted to VidFlow's developers

You are responsible for securing your own server infrastructure, including access controls and firewall configuration.

5. Third-Party Platforms

VidFlow integrates with the following third-party platforms. Your use of these platforms — including any data they collect about your content and viewers — is governed by their own privacy policies:

  • TikTok: TikTok Privacy Policy
  • YouTube / Google: Google Privacy Policy
  • Meta (Facebook / Instagram): Meta Privacy Policy

6. Data Retention

Video files downloaded for processing are deleted from your server after successful upload to the target platforms (typically within minutes to hours). Authentication tokens are retained on your server until you revoke them through the respective platform's account settings or delete them from VidFlow's configuration files.

7. Your Rights

You may at any time:

  • Revoke platform access tokens through each platform's account settings (TikTok, YouTube, Facebook, Instagram)
  • Delete your local VidFlow database and configuration files to remove all stored data
  • Contact us (see Section 10) to ask questions about how your data is processed

8. Children's Privacy

VidFlow is not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect or process information from minors. If you believe a minor is using VidFlow, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in the Service or applicable law. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of VidFlow after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.
